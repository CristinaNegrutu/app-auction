Our Services

AppAuction is a platform that helps small business or individuals that need software projects for their products find developers willing to build the apps for them according to their time and budget.

Advantages for clients

Post the description of the application that you need and choose among the bids of the developers.

Advantages for developers

Do you have some extra time and experience? This is the place for you. Choose among the proposed projects based on your skill and time. When you find the right project, bid an offer for the client by telling them the price and the time that you need to build it.

Transparency

Developers can see offers proposed by other developers so they will know how to adjust their own offer.

Safety

Payments and code delivery will be made via the platform.

It's a win-win

The business is only over when the client is happy with the result and the developers receives the payment.

Working on everybody's terms

Since the beginning of the collaboration, both parties must agree with each other's rules and requirements in order to avoid potential misunderstanding.
